About Me

My journey, passion, and what drives me

A Developer with a Passion for Innovation

Hello! I am MD Istehad Ahmed Rafi, a web developer based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. My journey into programming began with a deep fascination for how technology can solve real-world problems, leading me to pursue a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering from Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology (Dec 2020 - Dec 2024).

I thrive on challenges and am particularly excited by the intersection of web technologies with Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. My academic background and practical experience have equipped me with strong skills in Node.js, MongoDB, ASP.NET MVC, and Object-Oriented Programming. I believe in writing clean, maintainable code and am always eager to learn new tools and methodologies to deliver high-quality products.

This portfolio is a reflection of my dedication and the skills I have honed. Feel free to explore my projects and get in touch if you have an exciting opportunity or just want to connect!
